# Golden Son

## Overview

Golden Son is the second book in the Red Rising trilogy by Pierce Brown. It continues the story of Darrow as he navigates the complex political landscape of the Society.

## My Thoughts

The book delivers intense action sequences and deep character development. The political intrigue keeps you engaged throughout, and the stakes feel higher than ever.

## Key Themes

- Power and corruption
- Loyalty and betrayal
- Revolution and change

## Rating

A compelling read that expands on the world-building of the first book while raising the stakes significantly.
