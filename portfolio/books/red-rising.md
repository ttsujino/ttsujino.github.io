# Red Rising

## Overview

Red Rising is a dystopian science fiction novel set in a future where humanity is divided into a rigid color-coded caste system. The story follows Darrow, a Red miner who infiltrates the elite Gold society.

## My Thoughts

This book combines elements of The Hunger Games with Roman mythology and space opera. The world-building is exceptional, and the character development is compelling.

## Key Themes

- Class warfare and inequality
- Identity and transformation
- Sacrifice and revenge
- Power structures

## Why I Recommend It

If you enjoy dystopian fiction with complex characters and high stakes, this is a must-read. The action is relentless and the emotional impact is significant.
